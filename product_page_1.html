Koki Kola

Inspired by coca cola, this design exhibits a greenwashing in the form emphasizing a 'single environmental attribute while ignoring other impacts' (Nations). Coca cola is spending millions making claims of new innovation technologies by including 25% marine plastic within their bottles. This does not however inhibit nor do they mention, the fact that they are the worlds biggest plastic polluter (Laville).

It is important to note however that this design does slightly contribute positively to the environment by incorporating the recycled waste. However referring back to their exaggeration, this positive impact was only made for 300 bottles. Though it makes sense to not explicitly express the pollution contribution, as it goes against the interest of the company, it is also important that excessive amounts of money is not being put into deceiving the consumers.
