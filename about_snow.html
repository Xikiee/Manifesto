About Snow

Snow is a fake brand that is taken as an example of how real companies package and show their products in order to appeal to the more sustainable woke society. Snow is taken as a metaphor for greenwashing, creating a false sense of serene and beauty when in reality what is underneath has not changed. The soft palate continues this theme.

Did you find us through our social media posts? The posts were made for a call to the new generation of designers growing up in a society that prioritizes sustainability more and more. This way a larger audience can be reached. The website is used as an interactive medium in the hopes to maintain the attention of the audience and explain the idea in greater detail while also providing examples. The HTML code is converted to a real domain made to be accessible online by anyone rather than a file in a folder to allow for online engagement. We aspire to fester a community of designers that truly care about design and sustainability and can help inspire other designers. You are the new generation and you can make an impact even in large companies. Join us by submiting your desing ideas to o.l.lohr@student.tue.nl.
